Marketing

With the introduction of modern technologies and the importance of digital marketing (DM), businesses are doing all they can to speed them up. Businesses are either changing their plans in digital action or increasing existing marketing strategies with digital advertising techniques. Without marketing, even if your business can offer excellent products or services, your potential customers will not have any information about it.



SEO & Online Marketing


Help future customers find you on Google and Bing. As a digital marketing agency, we offer search engine optimization services to help you rank your website for the most important keywords for your business. Every day, millions of people search for the products or services you business offers - we make sure they see your brand when they do.



Social Media Marketing


By advertising on social media, we take advantage of platforms to connect your product or service to millions of people. We use highly targeted methods of social media advertising that allow our customers to point out their exact demographic and reach them at cheaper rates. Along with our social media services, we will create ads, create goals, and optimize them for the best results.



Content Marketing


Our exceptional content marketing services focus on developing relevant content to engage your audience. This will increase traffic to your website and accelerate your customer travel cycle. Working with your digital marketing agency for content is a great way to bridge the gap between your email marketing and social media marketing efforts on your website.



PPC/Google AdWords


People are searching for your products or services online now. They are using platforms like Google, Bing, and Yahoo to find relevant companies that cater to their needs. Search Engine Marketing (PPC) allows you to run ads on Google and other search engine platforms that show your brands when people are searching for the products and services you offer to them.




Online Reputation Management


With online reputation management strategies, you can improve the online presence of your brand. ORM helps by upgrading your website's ranking, ratings, social media, and search profiles. ORM is a process that involves strategies and techniques that allow your consumers to find information about your brand while they are searching for you over the Internet.



Video Marketing


The video is the ideal format for content consumption, 87% of marketers incorporate video into their content strategies. In Inesh, we use video in every phase of the sales channel, because it is the most efficient, effective way of conveying messages, understanding complex ideas and convincing stakeholders. Adding a video to your marketing plan is always a smart move.



Email Marketing


Email has become the biggest part of people's lives today and almost everyone has their own email address. Our email marketing strategies will help you to enhance your sales cycle. Our email marketing experts will develop striking email newsletters and automation successions to make your visitors more likely to buy from your business as compared to others.



Lead Generation


When it comes to generating potential leads, digital marketing becomes an important aspect. Lead generation essentially means promoting specific services pertaining to your business. This helps your business to show up on the search engines whenever a user searches for a product or service provided by you. This helps in driving more traffic & leads for your website and business.
